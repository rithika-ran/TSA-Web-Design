Our Mission

Lets make life a little easier for those busy parents


Bergen Help's goal is to create a resource hub to bring easy access to numerous different organizations and ammedities, catering specifically towards the needs of children. Rather it's afterschool care programs, tutoring services, or even pediatricians; We have it all. Our misson is to eliminate the barriers to essential services by making public resources clear, accessible, and easy to navigate for every family in our community. Through a compiled, and constantly updating list of resources, we hope to make the busy lives of families, just a little bit easier.


Through this resource hub, we strive to make life a little more convenient. Often times, scouring through the web to find programs can feel overwhelming and difficult to navigate. We use simple and accessible designs, to make it easier for users, while still keeping things bright and playful. Our goal is to create a platform that empowers our community.


- The Bergen Help Team
